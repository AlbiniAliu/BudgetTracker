Frequently Asked Questions

Adding a New Goal:
Go to the Goals section of the website.
Click on the "Add Goal" button.
Enter the goal name, target amount, and deadline.
Optionally, write notes or upload a relevant image for your goal.
Click "Save" to add the goal.
Adding a New Transaction:
Navigate to the Transactions section. Fill out the form in the "Add New Transaction" section: Enter the transaction name, description, amount, and date. Choose the transaction type (e.g., Income or Expense). Click the "Add Transaction" button. The transaction will appear in the transaction history table, and your overview will update automatically.

Go to the Profile section of the website.
Click the "Upload Profile Picture" button (or a similar option).
Select an image file from your device (JPEG, PNG, etc.).
Preview the image to confirm your choice.
Click "Save" to update your profile picture.
The new picture will be displayed across the platform.

Visit the Dashboard section to view an overview of your financial health.
Check the following:
Savings Goals Progress: Displays your progress for each goal using visual graphs or progress bars.
Transaction Analytics: Shows trends in your income and expenses over time.
Milestone Tracking: Highlights achievements like completed goals or specific savings targets.
Use filters or the search feature to focus on specific categories or time periods.

Go to the Settings or Account section.
Click on the "Change Password" or "Reset Password" option.
Enter your current password for verification.
Enter and confirm your new password.
Click "Save" to apply the changes.
If you forgot your password:
Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page.
Enter your registered email address.
Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions.
